The Great Game

The whistle blows and the game starts. The ball is crossed over to Bryan Mbumbo. He shoots — he scores!
But wait, why is nobody celebrating? Bryan looks back and realizes that Virgil and Mac Alister collided. Everyone is wondering if the goal counts: the home side is screaming for it to be canceled, the away stand is screaming for it to count. Finally, the referee makes the decision — Goooooaaaaal! The away stand erupts in celebration while the home side is speechless.
In the dying moments of the game, Mo Salah crosses it to Gakpo, who scores. Manchester United fans are worried, Liverpool fans are cheering.
Just when everyone thinks the game will end in a draw, Harry Maguire heads it in — 2-1! Manchester United wins. Fans scream Goooooaaaaal! It’s like nothing they’ve seen before.
As the final whistle blows, Manchester United fans and players celebrate their hearts out. That’s the story of the game.
